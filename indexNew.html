Alex Ginanjar is an Indonesian photographer known for his ability to blend artistic vision with compelling visual storytelling. Born and raised in Bandung, he has explored various photography genres, from portraiture and landscapes to documentary work. His photos often draw inspiration from the rich diversity of Indonesia’s nature and culture, making him one of the photographers who can truly capture the essence of Indonesia through his lens. Beyond commercial projects, Alex actively participates in both local and international photography exhibitions.
